##process
📊 **Excited to Share My Interactive Café Sales Dashboard! ☕**

I recently worked on building this **Interactive Café Sales Dashboard** as part of my learning journey in data analytics. This project helped me practice not only visualization but also the **complete data preparation and dashboard-building process**.

🔧 **What I worked on in this project:**

✅ **Data Cleaning:** Organized and cleaned the raw dataset to ensure accurate analysis.
✅ **Data Preparation:** Structured the data and created proper tables for better reporting.
✅ **Interactive Slicers:** Added slicers for **months and days** so users can easily filter and explore the data.
✅ **Dashboard Design:** Built a visually engaging and easy-to-understand layout.

📈 **Key Insights Displayed in the Dashboard:**

* **Item-wise Sales** to see the most popular café products
* **Month-wise Sales Trends** to understand seasonal patterns
* **Location-wise Sales** (In-store vs Takeaway)
* **Day-wise Sales Analysis** to identify the busiest days
* **Payment Method Distribution**
* **Quantity Distribution** to analyze order sizes
* **Key KPIs:** Total Transactions, Total Quantity, and Revenue
